About Dhruva Academy

Building strong fundamentals and problem-solving skills for competitive excellence

Dhruva Academy is a result-oriented coaching institute based in Patna dedicated to building strong fundamentals and problem-solving skills for school and competitive exams. We deliver structured curricula, regular tests, and personalized doubt-clearing sessions.

With experienced faculty and small batch sizes, each student receives targeted mentoring to maximize performance in JEE (Main & Advanced), NEET, board exams (CBSE/ICSE) and national Olympiads. Our competitive environment ensures every student reaches their full potential.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about our programs

What courses do you offer?
We offer Foundation courses (Class VI–X), Regular programs (Class XI–XII) for JEE/NEET, Crash courses, Test series, and Olympiad preparation including NTSE, PRMO, and IMO.
How can I enroll my child?
Contact us at +91 90654 13935 or visit our center at 2/22, S.B.I. Colony, Bailey Road, Patna. Our counseling team will guide you through the admission process.
Do you provide online classes?
Yes, we offer both online and on-site classes. Students can choose the mode that suits them best, with the same quality of teaching in both formats.
What is the batch size?
We maintain small batches of 20-30 students to ensure individual attention and personalized mentoring for each student.
Can students join crash courses only?
Yes, crash and revision batches are available before major exams. These intensive programs focus on quick revision and problem-solving.
What facilities are available?
We offer wheelchair-accessible seating and toilets, gender-neutral facilities, free parking, and both online and on-site service options. We are LGBTQ+ friendly.
