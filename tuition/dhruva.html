Who We Are

Dhruva Academy is a result-oriented coaching institute based in Patna dedicated to building strong fundamentals and problem-solving skills for school and competitive exams. We deliver structured curricula, regular tests, and personalized doubt-clearing sessions.

Our Approach

With 25+ experienced faculty and small batch sizes, each student receives targeted mentoring to maximize performance in JEE (Main & Advanced), NEET, board exams (CBSE/ICSE) and national Olympiads. Our competitive environment ensures every student reaches their full potential.
