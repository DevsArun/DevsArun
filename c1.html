World-Class Facilities

State-of-the-art infrastructure designed to nurture holistic development and excellence

üì±

Smart Classroom

Meet Technology, the presiding member of the Vidya Niketan School classroom. We have been successful in bringing technology to our classrooms with the introduction of interactive Whiteboards or Smart Boards.

These allow students to interact with the lesson content while allowing teachers to integrate a range of resources with their lessons. The classroom transforms into an interactive forum for sharing experiences among students, thus helping in widening their analytical faculties.

Classrooms are spacious, well-lit and have aesthetically designed furniture, keeping in mind the comfort of the child.

02
üî¨

Science & Computer Labs

Computer Lab: A hi-tech computer lab to make the students tech-savvy and to keep them abreast with the latest techniques that teaches not just computer as a subject, but also, the integration of computer skills for learning of other subjects in the curriculum.

Science Lab: The school supports an education system that emphasizes experiential learning. With well equipped and state of the art laboratories, students are provided opportunities to question, explore, examine, hypothesize and draw inferences thus building a scientific temperament.

Language Lab: Houses state-of-the-art equipments with facilities for audio and video recordings useful for honing students' language skills.

03
üìö

Library

Information is the heartbeat of meaningful learning in schools. At Vidya Niketan School, our library creates a learning environment that empowers students to connect with, engage with and utilize information in meaningful and purposeful ways.

We have developed strong library and information services which are essential to a learning program that values the cognitive, cultural, social, affective, technological and spiritual development of students in a globalized, multicultural and uncertain world.

Librarians are actively engaged in the development of curriculum across all learning areas to ensure the integration of information skills.

04
üé®

Art Room

Our dedicated Art Room provides a creative sanctuary where young artists can explore various mediums and techniques. The room is equipped with easels, art supplies, and inspiring displays of student work.

Students learn painting, drawing, sculpture, and crafts under the guidance of experienced art teachers. The curriculum focuses on developing creativity, fine motor skills, and artistic expression.

Regular art exhibitions and competitions encourage students to showcase their talents and build confidence in their creative abilities.

05
üéµ

Music & Dance Room

Our state-of-the-art Music and Dance Room is acoustically designed to provide the perfect environment for learning performing arts. Students have access to various musical instruments including keyboards, tabla, guitar, and harmonium.

The dance section features a spacious practice area with mirrors and professional sound systems. Students learn both Indian classical and contemporary dance forms.

Regular performances, cultural events, and inter-school competitions help students develop confidence and stage presence while celebrating artistic expression.

06
üßò

Yoga & Aerobics

Recognizing the importance of holistic health, we have dedicated spaces for Yoga and Aerobics. Our trained instructors conduct daily sessions focusing on physical fitness, mental well-being, and mindfulness.

Students learn various yoga asanas, pranayama techniques, and meditation practices that help improve concentration, flexibility, and overall health.

The aerobics program includes fun, age-appropriate exercises that promote cardiovascular health and develop a lifelong habit of staying active and healthy.

07
‚öΩ

Playground & Sports

Our expansive playground provides ample space for various outdoor activities and sports. The facility includes courts for basketball, volleyball, and badminton, along with a dedicated area for cricket and football.

Students participate in regular physical education classes, inter-house competitions, and district-level sports events. Our experienced coaches help identify and nurture sporting talent.

The playground also serves as a venue for morning assembly, annual sports day, and other school events, fostering team spirit and sportsmanship among students.

08
üè•

Medical Room

Student health and safety is our top priority. Our well-equipped medical room is staffed with a qualified nurse and has tie-ups with nearby hospitals for emergency situations.

The facility includes first-aid equipment, essential medications, and a comfortable resting area. Regular health check-ups, dental camps, and eye examinations are conducted throughout the year.

We maintain detailed health records for each student and ensure parents are immediately informed in case of any medical concern or emergency.

09
üçΩÔ∏è

Cafeteria

Our spacious and hygienic cafeteria provides nutritious and delicious meals prepared under strict quality standards. The menu is carefully designed by nutritionists to ensure balanced and healthy meals for growing children.

The cafeteria maintains high standards of cleanliness and food safety. We use fresh ingredients and follow proper cooking practices to ensure every meal is both tasty and nutritious.

Students can enjoy their meals in a comfortable, well-ventilated dining area that accommodates the entire school during lunch hours.

10
üöå

Transport Facility

We provide safe and comfortable transportation services covering all major areas of Obra, Aurangabad and surrounding regions. Our fleet of well-maintained buses ensures students reach school safely and on time.

Each bus is equipped with GPS tracking, first-aid kits, and fire extinguishers. Trained drivers and female attendants accompany students on every route, ensuring their safety and comfort throughout the journey.

Parents can track bus locations in real-time through our mobile app, providing peace of mind and transparency in transportation services.
