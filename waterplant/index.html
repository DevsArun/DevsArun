Our Process

From Source to Your Glass

LUXURY H₂O follows a rigorous 6-step water purification process — inspired by global water treatment plant standards — to ensure every drop is safe, pure, and mineral-enriched.

1

Raw Water Collection

Sourced from deep groundwater and protected natural sources in Bihar, free from surface contamination.

2

Multi-Stage Filtration

Sand filters, activated carbon filters, and micron cartridge filters remove suspended particles and impurities.

3

RO Purification

Reverse Osmosis membrane removes dissolved salts, heavy metals, bacteria and viruses at a molecular level.

4

UV Sterilization

Ultra-Violet light kills 99.99% of remaining microorganisms — ensuring biologically safe, germ-free water.

5

Mineral Enrichment

Essential minerals like Calcium, Magnesium, and Potassium are reintroduced to make the water healthy and great-tasting.

6

Hygienic Packaging

Packed in a dust-free, air-filtered clean room facility with tamper-proof seals — from 250ml bottles to 20L jars.
