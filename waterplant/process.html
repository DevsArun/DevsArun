Step by Step

6-Stage Purification Journey

From raw groundwater to your bottle — every step is carefully controlled, tested, and verified before LUXURY H₂O reaches your hands.

01
Stage

Raw Water Collection

Source Stage

Water is sourced from deep protected groundwater bores in Padrauna, Bihar — far below surface level where contamination risk is minimal. The source is regularly tested for natural quality before entering the plant. Only approved water enters Stage 2.

Deep Groundwater Source Quality Test Protected Bore
02
Stage

Multi-Stage Pre-Filtration

Physical Filter

Raw water passes through a series of pre-filters: Sand Filter (removes sediment & suspended particles), Activated Carbon Filter (removes chlorine, organic compounds & odour), and 5-Micron Cartridge Filter (removes fine particles before RO). This protects the RO membrane and ensures longevity.

Sand Filter Activated Carbon 5-Micron Cartridge Sediment Removal
03
Stage

Reverse Osmosis (RO)

Core Purification

High-pressure RO membrane with 0.0001 micron pore size removes 99% of all dissolved solids including heavy metals (lead, arsenic, mercury), fluoride, nitrates, pesticides, dissolved bacteria and viruses. Water is forced through the semi-permeable membrane — only pure H₂O molecules pass through.

0.0001 Micron Membrane 99% TDS Removal Heavy Metal Removal High-Pressure System
04
Stage

UV Sterilization

Bio-Kill Stage

Post-RO water flows through a UV sterilization chamber. UV light at 254nm wavelength destroys the DNA/RNA of any remaining microorganisms — bacteria, viruses, cysts, and protozoa — making them permanently inactive. This stage adds zero chemicals and has zero taste impact.

254nm UV Wavelength 99.99% Germ Kill No Chemicals DNA Destruction
05
Stage

Ozone (O₃) Treatment

Final Disinfection

Ozone gas (O₃) is dissolved into the purified water — oxidizing any trace remaining contaminants, eliminating all odour and improving taste clarity. Ozone is 3000x more powerful than chlorine and breaks down into plain oxygen after treatment — leaving absolutely zero residue. It also extends the shelf life of LUXURY H₂O naturally.

3000x Stronger than Chlorine Zero Residue Odour Elimination Extended Shelf Life
06
Stage

Clean Room Packaging

Final Stage

Purified water is filled and sealed in a dust-free, air-filtered clean room under strict hygiene protocols. Bottles and jars are sanitized before filling. Tamper-evident seals are applied immediately. Every batch is labelled with production date and tested before dispatch — ensuring the purity that reached Stage 6 stays intact until you open it.

Dust-Free Clean Room Tamper-Proof Seal Batch Quality Check Date Labelled
