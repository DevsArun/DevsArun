Advanced Technology

RO + UV + Ozone Purification

LUXURY H₂O uses triple-stage advanced purification technology — the gold standard in modern water treatment plants worldwide.

Reverse Osmosis (RO)

High-pressure RO membrane removes dissolved salts, heavy metals, fluoride, arsenic, pesticides, and bacteria at a molecular level — down to 0.0001 microns.

Removes 99% TDS
Eliminates heavy metals
Kills bacteria & viruses

Ultra-Violet (UV)

UV light at 254nm wavelength destroys the DNA of microorganisms — eliminating 99.99% of bacteria, viruses, cysts, and pathogens without adding any chemicals to the water.

99.99% germ elimination
No chemicals added
Safe for all ages

Ozone (O₃) Treatment

Ozone is nature's most powerful disinfectant — 3000x stronger than chlorine. It eliminates any remaining trace contaminants, deodorizes water, and extends shelf life naturally.

3000x stronger than chlorine
Removes odour & taste
Extends shelf life
