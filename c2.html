Eligibility

IIBF JAIIB Exam 2026 May: Eligibility

1

The examination is open only to the ordinary members of the Institute.

2

Candidates must have passed the 12th standard examination in any discipline or its equivalent.

3

The subordinate staff of recognized Banking/ Financial Institutions in India, who are members of the Institute, are eligible to appear at the examination, provided they have passed the 12th standard examination or its equivalent.

JAIIB Exam May 2026: Passing Criteria

The minimum mark to qualify for each paper of JAIIB was 50 out of 100.

Candidates securing at least 45 marks in each subject with an aggregate of 50% marks in all subjects in a single attempt will also be declared as having passed.

There is NO NEGATIVE marking in the JAIIB Exam.

Credits

JAIIB Exam May 2026: Credits for Subjects Passed under Revised Syllabus

If a candidate already passed Paper 1 (PPB) & Paper 2 (AFM) in the previous syllabus (within 4 attempts and 2 years span), he/she will get the credit in Principles & Practices of Banking & Accounting & Financial Management for Bankers. He/she need not appear in these two papers under the revised syllabus in May 2026.

Time Limit

JAIIB Exam May 2026: Time Limit for Passing JAIIB

A candidate will be given 5 attempts for completion of the JAIIB exam but, within a maximum period of three years, whichever is earlier, from the time he/she registers for the exam. These 5 attempts need not be consecutive.

Candidates unable to pass the exam within the max stipulated time period of 3 years or 5 attempts, whichever is earlier, will be required to re-enrol themselves afresh. Such candidates will not be granted credit/s for subject/s passed, if any, earlier.

Time limit of 3 years or 5 attempts, whichever is earlier, will start from the date of registration for the first attempt. Attempts will be counted when he/she applies for the exam, irrespective of whether the candidate appears for any examination or not.

JAIIB Notification May 2026: Download JAIIB Admit Card 2026

The Admit Card for the JAIIB 2026 exam is expected to be available for download approximately one week before the scheduled exam date. Candidates can access their admit card online through the official website of the Indian Institute of Banking and Finance (IIBF). It's essential to possess the JAIIB 2026 Hall Ticket to gain entry to the JAIIB Examination.

To ensure a seamless experience on the day of the exam, candidates are strongly advised to secure their JAIIB Admit Card well in advance, preferably before the IIBF's specified closing date. Having a printed copy of the admit card on hand before the exam is also recommended.

The JAIIB Admit Card is a mandatory document that must be brought to the examination center.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. Have the JAIIB 2026 Nov Exam Dates been declared?
No, the JAIIB 2026 Nov Exam is to be declared soon.

Q2. Is the JAIIB Registration for 2026 being opened?
No, the JAIIB Registration for May 2026 will be open from March 1st to Aug 21st, 2026.

Q3. What does JAIIB stand for?
JAIIB stands for Junior Associate of the Indian Institute of Bankers.

Q4. Who is eligible to apply for JAIIB 2026?
The JAIIB exam is specifically intended for individuals employed in the banking and finance sector who are also members of the Institute.

Q5. What is the eligibility criterion for the JAIIB exam?
To be eligible for the JAIIB exam, candidates must work in the banking and finance sector and be regular members of the Indian Institute of Banking and Finance (IIBF).

Q6. How do I register for the JAIIB 2026 exam?
Candidates must visit the IIBF's official website and complete the online registration process within the specified timeframe. The registration link will be accessible on the website during the registration period.

Q7. When will the JAIIB 2026 results be declared?
The precise date will be provided by the IIBF on their official website. Typically, results are announced a few weeks after the completion of the exam.

Q8. Can I apply for both the June and May sessions of JAIIB 2026?
Yes, eligible candidates can apply for both the June and May sessions of JAIIB 2026. The exam is conducted twice a year, allowing candidates to appear in both sessions if they wish.

Q9. Is there negative marking in the JAIIB exam?
No, there is no negative marking in the JAIIB exam. Marks are awarded only for correct answers, and there are no deductions for incorrect answers.
