FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with BeatMint

What services do you offer? +

We offer comprehensive D2C growth solutions including Shopify eCommerce websites, SEO optimization, digital ads (Facebook/Google), social media management, graphic/video design, influencer marketing with 1200+ verified creators, and strategic business guidance for D2C founders looking to scale.

How can I get a free consultation? +

Simply fill out the multi-step consultation form on this page, or contact us directly through WhatsApp, phone, or email. We'll schedule a free 30-minute strategy call to understand your business goals and recommend the best growth path for your D2C brand.

What is your pricing model? +

Our pricing varies based on the services and scope of your project. We provide flexible, tailored packages to meet your specific needs and budget. After our free consultation, we'll share a detailed proposal with transparent pricing and deliverables. Most projects range from ₹15,000 to ₹2,00,000+.

How long does it take to build a Shopify website? +

Typically, a custom Shopify website takes 2-4 weeks to complete, depending on the complexity, features required, and your content readiness. Rush delivery options are available for an additional fee. We prioritize both speed and quality to get your store live quickly without compromising on design or functionality.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance? +

Yes! We offer comprehensive ongoing support and maintenance packages starting from ₹5,000/month. This includes website updates, security monitoring, performance optimization, bug fixes, content updates, and priority support. We're committed to ensuring your digital presence continues to perform optimally.

What makes BeatMint different from other agencies? +

Unlike traditional agencies, we specialize exclusively in D2C brands and understand the unique challenges of direct-to-consumer business models. Our integrated approach combines Shopify development, performance marketing, and influencer partnerships under one roof. Plus, we've successfully launched 300+ websites and managed 999+ campaigns with proven results.

Can you help with both new launches and existing brands? +

Absolutely! We work with brands at all stages - from pre-launch ideas to established businesses looking to scale. Whether you're starting from scratch or need to optimize your existing operations, our team has the expertise to guide you through every phase of D2C growth.

How do you measure success and ROI? +

We use comprehensive analytics and tracking tools to measure key metrics including website traffic, conversion rates, customer acquisition cost (CAC), return on ad spend (ROAS), engagement rates, and overall revenue growth. You'll receive detailed monthly reports with actionable insights and recommendations for continuous improvement.
