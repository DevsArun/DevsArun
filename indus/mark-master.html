Privacy Policy – Mark Master

Effective date: 23 July 2026  •  App: Mark Master (Tic Tac Toe Game)

This Privacy Policy describes how Mark Master ("the App", "we", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile game. By downloading or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1 Information We Collect

  • Account information: If you sign in with email and password, we collect your email address and the username you choose.
  • Guest accounts: If you play as a guest, an anonymous account is created that is not linked to your personal identity.
  • Game data: Match results, scores, coins, unlocked skins, and game history are stored to provide leaderboards, online multiplayer, and progress tracking.
  • Device information: Basic technical information (such as device type and operating system version) may be processed automatically by our service providers to keep the App working reliably.
We do NOT collect your contacts, photos, location, SMS, call logs, or any other sensitive personal data.

2 How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your player account
  • To run online multiplayer matches and global leaderboards
  • To save your game progress, coins, and skins
  • To maintain, secure, and improve the App

We never sell your personal information to anyone.

3 Third-Party Services

The App uses Google Firebase (Authentication and Realtime Database) to provide login and online game features. Firebase may process data as described in Google's privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

The current version of the App is 100% ad-free and does not show any third-party advertisements.

4 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers. Access to game data requires authentication, and we take reasonable technical measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or loss.

5 Account & Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated game data at any time by contacting us at the support email below. We will process valid deletion requests within a reasonable time. Guest accounts and their data may be removed automatically when no longer needed.

6 Children's Privacy

Mark Master is a simple board game suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond what is required for basic game functionality (such as a chosen username). If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will remove it.

7 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
