The Problem

Coaching Institutes Are Losing Students Every Day

These operational gaps silently drain revenue, waste staff time, and frustrate parents — costing you admissions you should have won.

Missed Admission Enquiries

Enquiries from WhatsApp, website, and calls go unanswered during off-hours. Every missed enquiry is a lost admission worth ₹30,000–₹1,00,000+.

~40% enquiries lost after hours

Staff Wasting Time on Repetitive Questions

Your team answers the same "What is the fee?", "When do classes start?" questions 50+ times a day — time that could be spent on conversions.

3+ hours wasted daily per staff

Manual Fee Reminders

Staff individually calling parents for fee payment is inefficient, uncomfortable, and often delayed — leading to mounting receivables.

₹5L+ in delayed collections/month

Lost Follow-Ups

Interested parents who don't convert on the first visit are never followed up — they join your competitor who called them back first.

60% leads never get a 2nd contact

Delayed Parent Responses

Parents expect instant replies on WhatsApp. A delay of even 30 minutes makes them move on to another coaching institute.

Avg. response time: 4+ hours

No Central Data System

Student data scattered across Excel sheets, notebooks, and WhatsApp chats. No single source of truth for leads, students, or payments.

Zero visibility into pipeline
